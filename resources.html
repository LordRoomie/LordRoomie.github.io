The following links are unordered resources which are of high-quality and can help you study and prepare for exams.
  • NCEA Exam Resources and Past Papers
    • This is the official NZQA website and is a valuable resource specifically if you want to see exemplars and past papers of the standards you are sitting.
  • Khan Academy
    • Khan Academy while not specifically related to the standards many in New Zealand sit, it a high-quality and valuable resource which ought to be used to study in a multitude of subjects.
  • No Brain Too Small
    • No Brain Too Small is an excellent resource for Biology, Chemistry, and Physics subjects. It is specifically geared towards helping New Zealand students in studying for their NCEA Science standards.
  • Study Time NZ
    • Study Time NZ hosts many free resources covering subjects from English to Statistics. Including NCEA past papers and exam strategy guides.
